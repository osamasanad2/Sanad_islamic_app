PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

Sanad ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data (GPS) to calculate accurate prayer times and determine Qibla direction. Location data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.

Device Information: We collect anonymous device identifiers for analytics and crash reporting purposes. This helps us improve app stability and user experience.

Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how the app is used and to improve features. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Notification Permissions: We request notification permissions to deliver prayer time alerts, adhan notifications, and important reminders.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

- To calculate accurate prayer times based on your location
- To determine Qibla direction
- To deliver push notifications for prayer times
- To improve app performance and user experience
- To provide crash reporting and analytics

4. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Location data is never shared with third parties. Aggregated, anonymous analytics may be shared with service providers (Firebase, Crashlytics) solely for app improvement.

5. DATA RETENTION

Location data is processed locally on your device and is not retained by us. Analytics data is retained for a period of 12 months.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:
- Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging)
- Each service has its own privacy policy governing data collection.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Withdraw location permissions at any time through device settings
- Opt out of analytics through device settings
- Request deletion of any data we may hold
- Disable notifications through device settings

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app.

10. CONTACT

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
support@sanad.app
