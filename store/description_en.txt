Sanad is your complete Islamic companion app designed to enrich your spiritual journey. Featuring a beautiful, intuitive interface in both English and Arabic, Sanad brings together everything you need for your daily Islamic practice.

★ Quran Reader
Read the Holy Quran with a clear, elegant Mushaf-style display. Browse by surah or juz, bookmark verses, track your reading progress, and search the Quran with ease.

★ Accurate Prayer Times
Get precise prayer times based on your location using multiple calculation methods. Sanad automatically adjusts for your city and displays the remaining time until the next prayer with beautiful notifications.

★ Qibla Compass
Find the direction of the Kaaba from anywhere in the world with our accurate digital compass. The visual interface makes it simple to orient yourself for prayer.

★ AI-Powered Islamic Assistant
Ask questions about Islamic topics, get explanations of Quranic verses, learn about hadith, and explore the rich tradition of Islamic scholarship through our integrated AI assistant.

★ Adhkar & Daily Supplications
Access a comprehensive collection of morning, evening, and occasion-based remembrances with audio recitations and translations.

★ Audio Recitations
Listen to beautiful Quran recitations from renowned qaris. Download for offline listening and create your own playlists.

★ Islamic Calendar
View the Hijri calendar alongside the Gregorian date. Track important Islamic events and special days.

Sanad is completely free with no ads. We respect your privacy - your location data is used only for prayer time calculation and never shared with third parties.
